More about me

I am Christian Incrivaglia, a student in the fifth year of the diploma course in Graphic Design and Communication. My passion for visual art and creative communication guided me on my academic path, where I acquired skills in graphic design, illustration, photography, typography and digital marketing.
During my studies, I developed a strong aesthetic sense and an ability to combine creativity and digital techniques to create visual designs that capture attention and effectively communicate complex messages. I have worked on various projects, both individually and in teams, collaborating with other students and professionals to create work ranging from advertising graphics to the creation of complete visual identities.

My goal is to pursue a career in graphic design and communication, where I can continue to grow and contribute my innovative ideas and attention to detail. I am particularly interested in working in creative agencies, design studios, or as a freelancer, where I can apply my skills and continuously learn from new challenges.

I am always looking for new opportunities to challenge myself and expand my knowledge in the world of design and visual communication!
