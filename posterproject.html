Jordan Poster

The poster shows a tribute to legendary basketball player Michael Jordan. In the centre, an image of Jordan stands out as he is suspended in the air during an iconic dunk, wearing the classic red Chicago Bulls uniform with the number 23.

His face is determined, with an expression that captures the intensity of the moment. In the background, there is a large black-and-white portrait of Jordan's face, which adds depth and invokes the idea of his status as an iconic figure in the world of sport.

At the top, Nike's famous ‘Jumpman’ logo, stylised in red, is visible, further emphasising the link with the athlete. Here it is offered in 3 different versions, in my opinion the best being the central one.

Bagnaia Poster

This poster celebrates the success of Francesco ‘Pecco’ Bagnaia, rider of the Ducati Lenovo team in MotoGP, and was created to capture the highlights of his winning season. The design features a powerful contrast of deep reds and whites that recall Ducati's iconic colours, creating a sense of energy and dynamism.

The poster features four main images of Bagnaia: one in which he bites the medal, the symbol of his victory, another showing him celebrating with his hand to his ear in jubilation, a third in action on his bike during a race and the fourth in the background of the first three, with his helmet on. The shattered effect surrounding the central images adds a touch of movement and intensity, emphasising the adrenaline and speed of MotoGP.

At the top left, the official MotoGP logo underlines the authenticity of the sporting context, while the name ‘Francesco Bagnaia’ and the number ‘1’ strategically placed on the right side confirm his status as champion. The team's main sponsors, Ducati and Lenovo, are prominently displayed, contributing to the team's identity and brand celebration.
This poster is a visual tribute to Bagnaia's talent and dedication, capturing the essence of competition and victory in MotoGP.
